FAQ

Is this a fact-checking label?

It’s closer to a verification and integrity signal: a standardized process + evidence trail. For complex claims, TruthVerify can point to sources, context, and review history rather than “declaring truth.”

Can verification be revoked?

Yes. Status can change (e.g., Verified → Under Review → Revoked) if evidence shifts or policy violations occur.

How should journalists or investors use this page?

As a quick trust snapshot: status, scope, and what was checked — plus the presence of an audit trail.
