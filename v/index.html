What happens next

A simple, public record is created (when production is live) with a tamper-evident ID and a shareable page.

Step 1
You submit a request (link + handle + what to verify).
Step 2
We capture evidence artifacts (source snapshot, metadata, hashes).
Step 3
We publish a record page: status + confidence + limitations.
Step 4
You share the record URL (bio, decks, or comments).
